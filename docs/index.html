Frequently Asked Questions

Does ZenMode collect my data?

Absolutely not. ZenMode runs entirely locally on your browser and does not communicate with any external servers.

How do I pause it?

Click the ZenMode icon in your browser toolbar. You can toggle specific features or click "Reset All" to return to defaults.

Which sites are supported?

Version 1.1 focuses on optimizing the YouTube experience. Support for more social media sites is coming soon.

Privacy Policy

Simple, transparent, and privacy-first.

1. No Data Collection

ZenMode operates entirely locally on your device. We do not collect, store, sell, or transmit any of your personal data, browsing history, or usage habits.

2. Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage capabilities (Chrome Storage API) solely to save your preferences (e.g., remembering that you want to hide Shorts). These settings never leave your computer.

3. Permissions

ZenMode requires "Host Permissions" for YouTube solely to execute the visual changes you requested (hiding elements). We do not view or modify your data on other websites.

Last Updated: 2025-12-07. This policy is effective as of the release of ZenMode v1.1.
